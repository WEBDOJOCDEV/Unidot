About Us

Unidot- A Pre-Press Facility

Unidot, established in 1997, in Chennai, India, is a pioneer in the business of processing photopolymer plates. Unidot has in-house facilities for colour separations and graphic manipulation, and is a complete pre-press house providing services to Flexo printers. It has also been engaged in the production and processing of pre-press products required for all types of printing like, photopolymer plates from the thinnest for computer stationery and label printers to the thicker grades for plastic bags, and the thickest for corrugated board printing.
